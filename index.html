Aakriti

Aakriti was born in New Delhi and brought up in Singapore. She had lived in three countries by the time she was one. Aakriti pursued her bachelors degree in chemical engineering from National University of Singapore. She has been a chemical engineer with Exxonmobil for more than four years. She moved to the US an year ago on a work assignment from Exxon. The first thing you notice about Aakriti is her shyness, which is deceptive, she is amicable and fun to spend time with. She has a soft smile, humble nature and calm personality. She likes to travel, read books and play piano. Aakriti listens well, gracefully expresses her opinions, has a good sense of humor and has strong family values.
-Vasu

Vasu

Vasu was born and brought up in New Delhi. After completing his Bachelors in Electronics & Communication Engineering from SRM University Delhi, he decided to explore the world beyond and went to Arizona State university to complete his Masters in Computer Science. For the last two years, he has made his home in Austin, Texas, working at NXP Semiconductors. Vasu is fun-loving, thoughtful and enjoys spending time with friends. His favourite pass time is to go for a long drive in his car with loud punjabi music blaring from the speakers. At the same time, he enjoys cooking (Dal Makhani is his signature dish!), travelling and running. Patient and considerate, he values his friends and family deeply and always strives to put their happiness first.
-Aakriti
